Who is Spider-Man

Spider-Man is a fictional superhero created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. He first appeared in the comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962.

Spider-Man's Powers

Spider-Man possesses abilities that include superhuman strength, quick reflexes and agility, plus the ability to adhere to almost any surface. His spider-sense alerts him to imminent dangers.
